Project:

Phsar Srae — Khmer agriculture marketplace.

Page purpose:

Deals page is for “Good Price” crop offers.

It shows time-limited farm deals, end-of-season supply, urgent harvest, and discounted crop offers.

Do NOT make it feel like rotten or bad-quality crops.

Make it feel like good opportunities for buyers and better income recovery for farmers.

Design:

Premium agriculture deals marketplace.

Clean white/cream background, green accents, warm orange/red for urgency, rounded cards, soft shadows, Khmer-first, product-focused.

Not dashboard.
